Welcome to Browser Sniper

Overview:

Browser Sniper is the realistic marksmanship simulator designed for practicing mental math.

How to Play:

To officially become a Browser Sniper, you must hit at least 8 out of 10 targets with the 15 rounds provided.

Users can adjust the elevation (vertical plane) and windage (horizontal plane) of their shot with the "Action Buttons." The horizontal plane has a direction and a value (in mils) to it.

Like any good sniper would have, you are provided a Range Card and a Wind Reference (at 500m) to assist in making the correct shot calls through the Action Buttons. You are also provided your DOPE (data on previous engagment) to assist in making shot adjustments.

Gameplay:

When the game begins, users will be provided the target information through the TGT DIST (target distance), WND SPD (wind speed), and WND DIR (wind direction) displays. Using the reference cards and shooting position picture as a guide, users will input their inputs into the Action Buttons section for their shot and then press the FIRE button when ready.

Inputs

The user will only have to input three values before hitting the FIRE button: elevation, windage direction, and windage. It is very important to note that elevation and windage (mils) take number inputs while the windage direction will only take three values: "left", "right", or "center"
