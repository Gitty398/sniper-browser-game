Browser Sniper

Overview

Browser Sniper is a realistic marksmanship simulator designed to practice mental math.

Objective:

Hit at least 8 out of 10 targets using 15 total rounds to qualify as a Browser Sniper.

Controls:

Use the Action Buttons to adjust:

  • Elevation (vertical, mils)
  • Windage Direction (left, right, or center>)
  • Windage (horizontal, mils)

Reference Tools:

  • Range Card
  • Wind Reference (500 m)
  • DOPE> (previous engagement data)

Use the reference tools to calculate your adjustments, enter your inputs, and press FIRE.

Inputs Required:

Before firing, enter:

  1. Elevation (number)
  2. Windage Direction (left, right, or center)
  3. Windage (number)
