Browser Sniper

Tips for Gameplay:

- Reference the Green Army Man image to identify the wind direction relative to the shooter.

- If the wind is coming from the shooter's right to left, then you will have to aim into the wind (right).

- The wind card displays values that are correct at 500m. The greater the distance your target is from 500m, the longer the wind will effect the round. The shorter the distance, the less wind effects.
